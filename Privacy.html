01
Who We Are

One Touch Parking Finder is a mobile web application that helps drivers find parking spots, EV charging stations, and navigate to them. The app is operated independently and is currently available as a Progressive Web App (PWA).

For any privacy-related questions, you can reach us at the contact details in Section 9.

02

03
How We Use Your Data

Your location is used for one purpose only: to query our parking and EV charging data API to return results near you. The coordinates are passed as query parameters in an HTTPS request, the results are returned, and the coordinates are not logged or retained by our backend.

All preferences you set (unit system, saved spots, search history, car pin) are stored exclusively in your browser's localStorage and never transmitted.

⚠️ Note: If you clear your browser data or uninstall the app, all locally stored preferences and saved spots will be deleted permanently as we have no server backup of this data.

04
Third-Party Services

The app makes requests to the following third-party services. Each has its own privacy policy:

  • OpenStreetMap / Nominatim — Used for address search geocoding and reverse geocoding your location into a readable address. Your coordinates are sent to their servers for this purpose. OSM Privacy Policy →
  • OSRM (Open Source Routing Machine) — Used to calculate walking and driving directions. Origin and destination coordinates are sent to compute routes. OSRM Project →
  • CartoDB / Carto — Provides the dark map tiles displayed in the app. Your approximate location may be inferred from tile requests. Carto Privacy Policy →
  • Open-Meteo — Used in the EV Planner to auto-fill current weather conditions. Your coordinates are sent to retrieve local weather. Open-Meteo Terms →
  • Google Maps / Apple Maps — Optionally opened when you tap the "Google Maps" or "Apple Maps" navigation buttons. These are separate apps subject to their own policies. We pass only the destination coordinates as a URL parameter.
  • Parking Operators (PayByPhone, HonkMobile, etc.) — Tapping payment buttons opens third-party apps via deep link. We pass no personal data; only a parking location identifier may be included in the link.
05
Data Storage & Security

All user-generated data (saved spots, search history, preferences, car pin) is stored in your browser's localStorage, which is sandboxed to the app's origin and inaccessible to other websites or apps.

All API communication between the app and our backend is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS. We do not maintain any database of user records, location history, or usage logs.

Our backend infrastructure is hosted on Vercel, which maintains SOC 2 compliance. However, since we do not store any personal data on our servers, there is no personal data at risk in the event of a server-side breach.

06
Your Rights & Choices

Because we do not collect or store personal data on our servers, most traditional data rights (access, correction, deletion requests) apply to data held only on your own device. You are always in control:

  • Location access — You can deny or revoke location permission at any time in your device or browser settings. The address search bar works without GPS.
  • Clear all local data — Clear your browser's site data for this app to erase all saved spots, search history, and preferences instantly.
  • Saved spots — Delete individual saved spots inside the app at any time using the trash icon.
  • Search history — Clears automatically with browser data, or when you clear the app's site storage.

If you are a resident of the EU (GDPR), California (CCPA), or Canada (PIPEDA), you have additional rights regarding personal data. As we collect no personal data on our servers, these regulations impose minimal obligations on us — but we are committed to full compliance regardless. Contact us with any questions.

07
Children's Privacy

One Touch Parking Finder is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If you believe a child under 13 has used the app in a way that transmitted personal data, please contact us immediately and we will investigate.

08
Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. For significant changes, we will display a notice within the app. Your continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

We will never make changes that retroactively compromise your privacy or introduce data collection practices without clear notice.
